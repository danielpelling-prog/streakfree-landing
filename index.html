Features

Everything you need to run your business

No more spreadsheets, sticky notes, or switching between apps. StreakFree brings it all together.

Smart Scheduling

Drag-and-drop scheduling with automatic route optimization. Set recurring jobs, manage frequency, and never double-book again.

Customer Management

Complete customer profiles with job history, payment records, special instructions, and communication logs all in one place.

Bank Connection

Connect your bank account securely via Open Banking. See payments as they arrive and match them to customers automatically.

Auto Payment Matching

Our AI learns your customers' payment patterns. Once you confirm a match, it remembers - future payments are matched automatically.

Card Payments

Take card payments on the spot or send payment links. Stripe integration means you get paid faster with lower fees.

Facebook Leads Integration

Connect your Facebook page and import leads directly into StreakFree. Never miss a potential customer from your ads or page enquiries.

Team Management

Add workers to your team, assign jobs, and let them access their schedule. They see only what they need - your data stays private.

Outstanding Balance Tracking

See exactly who owes you money at a glance. Chase payments with one click and keep your cash flow healthy.
